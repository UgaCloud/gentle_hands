Privacy Policy

At Gentle hands home care and services, your trust and the protection of your personal information are of utmost importance to us. This document explains our practices regarding the collection, usage, and safeguarding of your data when you interact with our website, engage our services, or reach out to us.

1. Updates to This Privacy Policy

We may revise this Privacy Policy periodically to align with evolving business practices or legal standards. Should any changes be made, the updated version will be published on this page, and the "Last Updated" date at the bottom will be revised accordingly.

2. Your Rights and Choices

You retain specific rights concerning your personal data, including the ability to:

  • Request access to, corrections for, or updates of your information.
  • Ask for the deletion of your personal data, recognizing that certain legal exceptions may apply.
  • Unsubscribe from any non-essential promotional messages we may send.

To exercise any of these rights, please utilize the contact methods provided at the end of this document.

3. Safeguarding Your Data

We employ reasonable and appropriate administrative, technical, and physical security measures to protect your personal information from loss, misuse, unauthorized access, disclosure, or destruction. Despite our best efforts, please be aware that no digital transmission or storage system can be guaranteed to be 100% secure.

4. When We Share Your Information

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to others. Your data may only be shared under the following circumstances:

  • With trusted external partners who assist us in website management, business operations, or service delivery, under strict confidentiality agreements.
  • When mandated by law, or if necessary to protect the legal rights, safety, or property of Gentle hands home care and services, our clients, or the public.

5. Use of Cookies

Our website utilizes cookies—small text files stored on your device—to remember your preferences and improve your overall browsing experience. You have the option to manage or disable cookies through your browser settings. However, please note that doing so may limit your access to certain features of our website.

6. External Website Links

Our site may provide links to external websites for your convenience or information. Please be aware that these third-party sites operate under their own privacy practices. We encourage you to review their policies before submitting any personal data, as we are not responsible for their content or privacy practices.

7. Information We Gather

To provide and improve our services, we collect information in the following categories:

  • Personal Identifiers: This includes details you knowingly provide, such as your full name, telephone number, and email address, when you contact us or request information.
  • Technical and Usage Data: When you browse our site, we may automatically collect data like your IP address, browser type, and pages visited. This helps us analyze trends, administer the site, and understand user behavior to enhance our service offerings.

8. Purpose of Data Collection

The information we collect is used for the following core purposes:

  • To deliver details about our home care offerings.
  • To answer your questions and address your concerns.
  • To analyze and enhance the functionality of our website and the quality of our services.
  • To meet applicable legal and regulatory obligations.
